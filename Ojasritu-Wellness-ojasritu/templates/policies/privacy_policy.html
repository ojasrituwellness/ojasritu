Privacy Policy

Updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

Ojasritu Wellness ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone number, password
  • Address Information: Billing and shipping addresses
  • Payment Information: Card details (processed securely by payment gateway)
  • Communication Data: Messages, inquiries, feedback, support tickets

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, OS, browser type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, clicks, interactions
  • Location Data: IP address, approximate location (not precise)
  • Cookies & Tracking: Session data, preferences, user identifiers

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Order Processing: To process, fulfill, and track your orders
  • Payment Processing: To securely process payments
  • Customer Support: To respond to inquiries and provide assistance
  • Marketing: To send promotional emails, offers, and newsletters (with consent)
  • Analytics: To understand user behavior and improve our services
  • Fraud Prevention: To detect and prevent fraudulent transactions

4. Security of Your Information

  • SSL Encryption: Website uses HTTPS for data transmission
  • Secure Payments: Payment details processed by secure gateways
  • Password Protection: Passwords hashed and encrypted
  • Access Control: Limited employee access to personal data
Note: While we implement security measures, no system is 100% secure. Use strong passwords and protect your account access.

5. Your Privacy Rights

A. Access & Portability

  • You can request a copy of your personal data
  • Data provided in a portable format within 30 days

B. Correction & Deletion

  • Update or correct your account information anytime
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Some data retained for legal/compliance purposes

C. Marketing Preferences

  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails anytime
  • Opt-out from personalized recommendations
  • Manage notification preferences in account settings

6. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained as long as account is active
  • After Account Deletion: Deleted within 30 days (some data retained for compliance)
  • Transaction Records: Retained for 6 years (for tax/legal purposes)
  • Marketing Data: Retained until unsubscription

7. Health Information Disclaimer

Important: Any health information provided (e.g., for Dosha assessment) is used solely for product recommendations and is protected under privacy laws. It is NOT used for medical diagnosis or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical advice.

8. Children's Privacy

  • Our website is not intended for children under 13 years
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13
  • If we discover such data, it will be deleted immediately

9. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of changes.
